Computer Ports

Where machines connect to the world.

USB Port

Used to connect external devices like keyboards, mouse, pen drives, and printers.

HDMI Port

Used for high-quality audio and video output to monitors and TVs.

Ethernet Port

Used to connect the computer to the internet using a wired network.

Audio Ports

Used for connecting headphones, speakers, and microphones.

VGA Port

An older display port used to connect monitors and projectors.

Type-C Port

A modern port used for fast data transfer, charging, and display output.
